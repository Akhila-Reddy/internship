REGISTRATION!

The candidate registers by providing a few basic details and if he/she is eligible to proceed to the next round then on completion of this process they get a unique registration id with which they can login for the next rounds.

SCREENING

This is the second round of the recruitment process where the candidate logs in with the unique id which has been provided to them after the registration after which he/she can participate in the online exam which is conducted at WISSEN. The candidates who excel in this round are promoted to the next round.
